HDB Renovation AI Assistant

Overview

The HDB Renovation AI Assistant is part of the Interior Design Chatbot Suite. It provides fast, accurate, and regulation-compliant renovation guidance for HDB homeowners in Singapore.

By leveraging Generative AI, the chatbot reduces confusion, delays, and uncertainty during renovation planning, allowing users to make informed decisions before engaging an interior designer.

Problem Addressed

Traditional interior design consultations are time-consuming and often require physical meetings. Many HDB homeowners are unsure about renovation rules, realistic budgets, renovation timelines, and suitable design concepts.

This results in delays, miscommunication, and poor renovation decisions. The HDB Renovation AI Assistant addresses these challenges by providing instant, property-specific guidance.

What the AI Can Help With

  • HDB renovation rules and permit requirements
  • Common do’s and don’ts for HDB flats
  • Realistic renovation budget ranges
  • Typical renovation timelines and workflows
  • Design ideas suitable for HDB layouts
  • Pre-qualification before meeting an interior designer

AI & Technology Used

This chatbot is powered by a Large Language Model (LLM) integrated using a chatbot flow platform. Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is applied to ground responses in HDB renovation guidelines, space planning principles, and realistic cost ranges.

The AI is designed to support users ethically by providing transparent, fact-based information and does not replace certified interior designers.
