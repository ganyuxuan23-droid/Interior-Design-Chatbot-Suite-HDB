HDB Renovation Rules

HDB flats have strict renovation rules to ensure safety and compliance. Examples include:
• No structural changes to main walls
• Soundproofing required for certain modifications
• Approval needed for bathroom and kitchen alterations

Budget & Pricing

Typical renovation costs for HDB flats range from $15,000 to $50,000 depending on design and scale. Costs include labor, materials, and furniture. Our AI helps you estimate and optimize your budget.

Design Tips

Minimalist, functional, and open layouts work best in HDB flats. Use light colors to maximize space perception, choose modular furniture, and consider hidden storage to declutter.

Renovation Timeline
